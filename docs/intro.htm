Audiotap is a program for conversion between .TAP files and samples of Commodore 64 tapes. Audiotap can read/write WAV files containing the sampled sound of a tape, and can also record tapes from the sound card and play sound to the sound card.

The latest version of WAV-PRG and Audiotap can be downloaded at the Sourceforge page, in the Files section
