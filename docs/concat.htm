From Explorer, select one or more .TAP (or .WAV, or .DMP as generated by a DC2N device) and drag them on the Audiotap window.
You will be asked to choose the output TAP file (you will also be able to choose the version of the TAP file, among 0, 1 or 2). When you've chosen, the conversion starts. Wait until the end, or press Cancel to stop immediately.
