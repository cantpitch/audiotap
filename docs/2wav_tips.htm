First of all, it is better to open the Volume Control (double click on the speaker in the system tray), then mute everything but Volume control and Wave. Set the volume of both to the maximum.

Connect the output of the sound card (the one normally connected to the speakers) to the input of a tape recorder (generally marked as AUX, CD, or PHONO). Since the sound at the recorder's input will be VERY loud, keep the volume of the recorder's speakers low.
